Academic Hub

Research Resources for PALASIK and PiTUSK

Reusable academic papers, methodological references, and citation-ready sources for follow-up studies.

Development Concept: PALASIK → PiTUSK

At the core of notedavidrinaldi is a practical edge progression:

  1. PALASIK establishes a real-time, trust-aware edge decision layer for security.
  2. PiTUSK adds reliability for data transport with resumable upload and state recovery.
  3. Together they form an end-to-end pattern: faster decisioning and data continuity under unstable networks.

The scientific objective behind this research hub is to document that progression as reusable evidence: how the owner’s expertise becomes a transferable academic contribution for IIoT edge reliability.

Owner Expertise as Research Focus

  • Design of trust-aware policy engines and auditable security decision workflows.
  • Building operational Go services for edge runtimes and transfer workflows.
  • Platform integration across constrained devices and industrial gateways.
  • Engineering discipline: instrumentation, reproducible metrics, and protocol-level validation.

Author/focus statement: to generate reusable academic resources that evaluate both security decision quality and reliability under real deployment constraints.

Purpose

This hub standardizes implementation-based references into a research-friendly format for external lecturers, students, and collaborators.

  • Research-style documentation for PALASIK and PiTUSK.
  • Methodological framework for future experiments.
  • Direct citation records and cross-paper mapping.
